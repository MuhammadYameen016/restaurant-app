The best restaurants in Sukkur are closer than you think


Some dyed-in-the-wool foodies never compromise. They travel miles to a restaurant renowned for biryanis (for example). And then travel some more, looking for the perfect falooda to wash it down.
Even if you’re not the travelling kind, you can still enjoy the culinary delights of the best restaurants in Sukkur by calling them directly and placing a food order, or by using the foodpanda link (more of that later) to have your favourite dishes sent to you.
However, with dozens of restaurants, roadside stalls, take-out joints and dhabas spread all over Sukkur, zeroing in on the ones to patronise might prove a bit of a feat. So why not simply study the following list of tried-and-tested establishments and then decide?

Grab a bite at the best!

✓ Apple Nine: located in Sitara Mall on the Main Boulevard, this pizza joint has won praise from its patrons for its pizzas, appetizers, salads and jaw-stretching sandwiches. Being spacious, it’s great for parties.

✓ McDonald’s: this restaurant chain needs no introduction. The Sukkur branch offers the usual fare, though it’s a bit pricey.

✓ Arabic Pizza Hut: what you can expect here is pizzas with an Arabian accent; more BBQ bits for toppings, for example.

✓ Sardines Restaurant: located on Canal Road, this eatery lists many Chinese, North Indian and Mughlai dishes on its menu. Its daily lunch+high tea is its greatest attraction.

✓ Quilim Chinese Restaurant: its lush interiors and lavish buffet make Quilim a must-visit place for any serious foodie.

✓ Real Taste Restaurant: a popular eating place for those with a passion for Mughlai fare. Large groups must book tables to avoid disappointment.

✓ Al Aziz Restaurant: as the name suggests, an eatery focused on Mughlai/Pakistani cuisine. The reshmi kebab and chicken archari are highly recommended.

✓ Baba Tikka: this establishment on Canal Road serves mainly Pakistani food. It offers home delivery if orders meet the minimum Rs500 limit and charges Rs100 for delivery.
